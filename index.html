🏆 Final recommendation

✅ BEST DESIGN: Modern Minimal (Design 1)

Why most practical: ₹15k–25k fits most Indian middle-class budgets, zero professional help, easy to clean (no velvet/silk), timeless neutral palette, and works for both small and medium bedrooms.

Why it looks best: Soft ivory walls + warm sconces create a calm, hotel-like atmosphere. Minimal furniture makes the room feel twice as large.

Why normal users should choose it: "One weekend, basic tools, no contractor — transform your cluttered bedroom into a calm, airy, modern sanctuary that stays practical for daily Indian life."

📝 Implementation checklist (Modern Minimal)

  • Empty the room completely – remove all movable items
  • Paint walls in Ivory White (2 coats, allow drying 24 hrs)
  • Remove old tube light fixture – install 2 warm LED wall sconces left & right of bed
  • Replace bed with low-profile wooden frame (or remove headboard for minimalist look)
  • Replace old curtains with light grey cotton floor-length curtains
  • Install 2 floating shelves (instead of bulky side tables)
  • Place one medium areca palm or snake plant near the window
  • Remove all visible clutter – use wardrobe + under-bed boxes
  • Add one large abstract print (black & white or earth tones)

🛠️ Pro tip: Use command hooks for light curtains and a basic drill for floating shelves. All items available on Amazon India / local furniture stores.
